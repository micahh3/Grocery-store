About Me

Hello my name is Micah. I am a 16yo digital and traditional artist with a love for unusualness and comedy. I love photography, drawing, watching movies, and music! I would say my artistic journey began when I was around 7 years old and loved painting and making comics.

I continued my artistic journey through middle school when I went Oakland School for the Arts (OSA) and joined the Media Arts department where I found my love for digital art and photography. Since attending OSA I have directed four short films/bumpers, found a love for animation and photography, and learned more about my artistic process. This site is a way for me to express, well me!

For now I just have this homepage and my digital art but I plan to create three more pages where I have a diary of the media (movies, shows, and youtube videos) that I consume, along with a page where I talk about musical artists I like and a page where I trash talk videogames I love playing.
